# Improve analytics & reporting

Enhance reporting capabilities for faster, more actionable marketing insights through unified dashboards and self-service tools. This objective focuses on providing teams with comprehensive, cross-channel analytics that enable faster identification of trends, opportunities, and issues.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Efficiency | Reduction in time required to generate and distribute marketing reports |
| Productivity | Increase in self-service analytics usage and report generation across teams |

The following use case patterns support this business objective.

| Pattern | Category | How it supports this objective |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Customer Analytics & Insight Generation](/help/blueprints/use-case-patterns/analysis/customer-analytics-insight-generation.md) | Analysis | Builds cross-channel analysis workspaces, computed metrics, and dashboards for comprehensive behavior and performance reporting |
