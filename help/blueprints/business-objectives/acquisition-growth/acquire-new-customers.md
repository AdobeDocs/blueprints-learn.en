# Acquire new customers

Expand the customer base through targeted acquisition campaigns, lookalike audiences, and paid media optimization. This objective focuses on reaching new prospects at scale while maintaining cost efficiency through precise audience targeting and suppression of existing customers.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| New Customers | Total count of newly acquired customers attributed to marketing activities |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | Total cost to acquire a new customer through paid and owned channels |
| Prospect/Lead Conversion | Percentage of prospects or leads that convert to customers |
