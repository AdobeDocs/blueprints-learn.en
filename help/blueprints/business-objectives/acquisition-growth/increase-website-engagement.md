# Increase website engagement

Improve time on site, pages per session, and interaction with web content through relevant experiences. This objective centers on delivering contextual, personalized web experiences that keep visitors engaged and progressing through content and conversion paths.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Time On Page | Average time visitors spend on key pages |
| Engagement | Composite score of interactions including clicks, scrolls, and content consumption |
| Conversion Rates | Percentage of engaged visitors who complete desired actions |
