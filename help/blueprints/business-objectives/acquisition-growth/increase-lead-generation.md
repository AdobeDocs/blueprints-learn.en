# Increase lead generation

Generate more qualified leads for the sales pipeline through forms, events, content, and multi-channel engagement. This objective focuses on growing the volume of qualified prospects entering the funnel through targeted outreach and optimized conversion paths.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Prospects | Total number of new prospects entering the pipeline |
| Cost Per Lead | Average cost to generate a qualified lead |
| Lead Conversion | Percentage of leads that progress to the next pipeline stage |
