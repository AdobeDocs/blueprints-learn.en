# Key business objectives

Key business objectives define the strategic outcomes that organizations seek to achieve through their digital experience initiatives. Each objective maps to one or more [use case patterns](/help/blueprints/use-case-patterns/overview.md) that describe how to implement Adobe Experience Platform and applications to deliver those outcomes.

Use the tables below to identify the business objectives most relevant to your organization, then follow the links to learn which use case patterns, KPIs, and implementation approaches support each objective.

## Acquisition & growth

The following objectives focus on expanding reach and growing the customer base.

| Objective | Description | Key KPIs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Acquire New Customers](acquisition-growth/acquire-new-customers.md) | Expand the customer base through targeted acquisition campaigns, lookalike audiences, and paid media optimization. | New Customers, Customer Acquisition Cost, Prospect/Lead Conversion |
| [Increase Lead Generation](acquisition-growth/increase-lead-generation.md) | Generate more qualified leads for the sales pipeline through forms, events, content, and multi-channel engagement. | Prospects, Cost Per Lead, Lead Conversion |
| [Increase Website Engagement](acquisition-growth/increase-website-engagement.md) | Improve time on site, pages per session, and interaction with web content through relevant experiences. | Time On Page, Engagement, Conversion Rates |

| Objective | Description | Key KPIs |

| [Increase Customer Loyalty & Lifetime Value](revenue-monetization/increase-customer-loyalty-lifetime-value.md) | Deepen customer relationships and maximize long-term value through loyalty programs, rewards, and personalized engagement. | Customer Lifetime Value, Retention, Upsell/Cross Sell % |
