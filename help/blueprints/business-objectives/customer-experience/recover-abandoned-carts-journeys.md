# Recover abandoned carts & journeys

Re-engage users who dropped off during purchase, application, or enrollment flows with timely, personalized follow-ups. This objective focuses on capturing lost revenue by detecting abandonment events in real time and delivering contextual messages that address barriers and incentivize completion.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Conversion Rates | Percentage of abandoners who return to complete the desired action |
| Incremental Revenue | Additional revenue recovered from abandoned cart and journey interventions |
| Engagement | Re-engagement rates from abandonment recovery messages |

The following use case patterns support this business objective.
