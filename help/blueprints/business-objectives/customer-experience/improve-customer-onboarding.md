# Improve customer onboarding

Accelerate time-to-value for new customers with streamlined, personalized welcome experiences and activation journeys. This objective focuses on guiding new customers through their initial experience with timely, relevant communications that drive product adoption and early engagement.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Engagement | Interaction rates during the onboarding period |
| Retention | Percentage of new customers who remain active after the onboarding period |
| Conversion Rates | Percentage of new customers who complete key activation milestones |

The following use case patterns support this business objective.
