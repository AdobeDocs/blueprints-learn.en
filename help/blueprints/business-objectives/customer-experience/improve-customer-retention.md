# Improve customer retention

Keep existing customers engaged and renewing through value-driven experiences and ongoing relationship nurturing. This objective focuses on identifying at-risk customers, delivering timely interventions, and maintaining ongoing engagement to reduce churn and sustain long-term relationships.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Retention | Percentage of customers who remain active over a defined period |
| Customer Lifetime Value | Total predicted revenue from a customer over their entire relationship |
| Engagement | Frequency and depth of customer interactions across channels |
