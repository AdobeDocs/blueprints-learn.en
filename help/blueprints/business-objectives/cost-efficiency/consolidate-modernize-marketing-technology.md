# Consolidate & modernize marketing technology

Reduce tool fragmentation and technical debt by migrating to unified, scalable platforms. This objective focuses on simplifying the marketing technology stack by consolidating point solutions into integrated platforms that share data, governance, and workflows.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Cost Savings | Reduction in total technology licensing and maintenance costs |
| Efficiency | Improvement in operational efficiency through unified platforms |
| Speed To Market | Faster time-to-launch for campaigns and experiences through streamlined tooling |
