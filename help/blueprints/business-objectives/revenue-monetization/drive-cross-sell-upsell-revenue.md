# Drive cross-sell & upsell revenue

Promote complementary and premium products or services to existing customers based on behavior and purchase history. This objective focuses on increasing customer value by presenting the right product recommendations and offers at the right time through personalized decisioning and multi-channel engagement.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Upsell/Cross Sell % | Percentage of customers who purchase additional or upgraded products |
| Incremental Revenue | Additional revenue generated from cross-sell and upsell activities |
| Customer Lifetime Value | Total predicted revenue from a customer over their entire relationship |
