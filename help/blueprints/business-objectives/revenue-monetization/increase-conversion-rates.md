# Increase conversion rates

Improve the percentage of visitors and prospects who complete desired actions such as purchases, sign-ups, or form submissions. This objective focuses on optimizing every touchpoint in the customer journey to reduce friction and drive action through personalized experiences and timely messaging.

## Key performance indicators

The following KPIs measure progress toward this objective.

| KPI | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Conversion Rates | Percentage of visitors or prospects who complete a desired action |
| Lead Conversion | Percentage of leads that progress through the funnel to conversion |
| Cost Per Lead | Cost efficiency of converting prospects into qualified leads |
